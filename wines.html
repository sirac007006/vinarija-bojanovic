Our Wines

Vranac "Balkanska Carica"

Vranac "Balkanska Carica" is a premium red wine that reflects the rich tradition and authenticity of the Podgorica subregion. Made from carefully selected grapes of the indigenous Vranac variety, this wine impresses with its intense ruby-red color, rich aromas, and full body.

Serving temperature: 16-18°C

Vranac Barrique "Balkanska Carica"

Vranac Barrique "Balkanska Carica" is a premium red wine that combines tradition and sophistication. Made from carefully selected Vranac grapes, this wine ages for 15 months in barrels made of the finest French oak, giving it a refined aroma and rich taste.

Serving temperature: 16-18°C

Bellezza Sauvignon Blanc

Bellezza is a dry white wine made from carefully selected Sauvignon Blanc grapes, offering freshness and aromatic character. Bellezza Sauvignon Blanc is an ideal choice for lovers of light and refreshing wines, perfect for warm summer days or paired with light gourmet specialties. The aroma features intense notes of citrus, green apple, and tropical fruits.

Serving temperature: 10-12°C

Bellezza Malvazija

Bellezza is a dry white wine made from the Malvasia Aromatica grape, grown in our family vineyard in the village of Beri. Bellezza Malvazija is an elegant and aromatic wine that reflects the richness of the Beri terroir. Its crystal-clear light yellow color with golden reflections hints at a refined character. The aroma features intense fruity notes of ripe pear, peach, and citrus.

Serving temperature: 10-12°C

Lozova Rakija Bojanović

Our premium Lozova Rakija is crafted through careful distillation of the finest grapes and aged at the ideal temperature for a rich and full taste. Crystal clear with refined fruity aromas and a smooth, lasting finish, this rakija offers an authentic taste of Montenegro's traditional spirit.
