Awards

Three Prestigious Awards from the Novi Sad Fair – A Testament to Excellence!

We proudly celebrate winning three prestigious awards at the Novi Sad Fair, reaffirming our dedication to premium quality and authentic wine flavors. Competing with numerous wineries, our products stood out with their exceptional aromas, richness of taste, and perfect balance. These awards are not just a recognition of our work but also a motivation to continue preserving tradition and crafting wines that captivate both experts and true wine lovers. Thank you for recognizing our effort – your support inspires us to be even better!

Awards at the Virpazar Wine and Ukljeva Festival!

Our winery proudly celebrates winning medals at the Virpazar Festival, a recognition of the quality, passion, and dedication we invest in every bottle. Among strong competition, our wine distinguished itself with its richness of taste, sophisticated aromas, and perfect balance. This award represents not only a great honor for us but also a motivation to continue creating wines that capture the hearts of both enthusiasts and experts. Thank you for your support – your trust is our greatest inspiration!

Gold Medal at the 1st International Spirits Festival in Slovenia!

Our distillery proudly celebrates winning the gold medal at the 1st International Spirits Festival in Slovenia, where our 2023 grape distillate was recognized for its exceptional quality. Among strong competition, we stood out with perfect purity, rich aromas, and an outstanding harmony of flavors. This award is not just an acknowledgment but also an incentive to continue producing distillates that impress both experts and true lovers of fine spirits. Thank you for your support – your trust is our greatest inspiration!
