Vinarija Bojanović

Beri-Podgorica

At the heart of tradition and flavor

A Journey Through the tradition

Located in the picturesque village of Beri near Podgorica, Vinarija Bojanović is a blend of family tradition, passion for viticulture, and the authentic flavors of the Montenegrin climate.
Our small winery nurtures a natural approach to winemaking, using indigenous and carefully selected grape varieties grown under the warm Mediterranean sun.
Each bottle of our wine tells a story of love for vineyards, hand-harvesting, and a meticulous production process, combining ancient wine techniques with modern methods to preserve the unique character of every bottle.
We are dedicated to quality and producing limited batches of wine, ensuring every sip provides true enjoyment for both local and international wine lovers.
At our winery, you can enjoy wine tastings, vineyard tours, and learning about the winemaking process in an ambiance filled with warmth and hospitality. Come and discover a unique wine experience in the heart of nature.

Wine Tasting

Enjoy flexible tours of our winery, tailored to your desires. Whether you prefer a short one-hour tasting or a full day immersed in wines and the vineyard atmosphere, we provide an unforgettable experience.

Classic Tasting

Tasting of 3-4 types of wine and two types of rakija with a brief introduction.

Premium Tasting

Tasting of 3-4 types of wine and two types of rakija, accompanied by Montenegrin prosciutto, cheese, and olives.
